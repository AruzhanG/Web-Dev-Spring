Travel to Paris

First Impression

After I landed in Paris, I was very excited and couldn’t wait to see the city. I don’t know—maybe I had just dreamed about France so much that I didn’t care about the huge amount of trash and rats.

Eiffel Tower

When I saw the Eiffel Tower, I was amazed by its size and beauty. The view from the top, with all of Paris below, was absolutely breathtaking. At night, when it sparkled, it felt like pure magic.
