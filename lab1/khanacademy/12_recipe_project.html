Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Egg yolks 4
Parmesan cheese 50g
Pancetta 100g
Salt & Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water.

Step 2: Mix egg yolks and grated Parmesan cheese in a bowl.

Step 3: Cook pancetta until crispy, then mix with the spaghetti and reserved water.

Step 4: Remove from heat, stir in the egg mixture, and season with salt and pepper.

Source: BBC Good Food

Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 3/4 cup
Egg 1
Butter 2 tbsp
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking powder 1 tsp

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.

Step 2: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.

Step 3: Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.

Step 4: Cook batter on a greased pan over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
